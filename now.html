My Life Today…

My life today at age eighty six is very full and very exciting. I am retired and can do as I please or come and go when ever the notion strikes me. Also, I can stay busy doing nothing and it is time well spent because no one expect people my age to do much anyway. I love to read and write. My computer, which I obtained in my later years when I became a widow, is one of my favorite past times. I stay busy with my many friends on the internet. God has blessed me with many wonderful friends both in the US and around the world. I enjoy both writing to them and hearing from them. I have come to love many people whom I have never met in person but have so much in common with.

I find great joy in teaching my Sunday school class of precious ladies. I love teaching God’s Word because it is so full of deep truths, exciting stories and daily lessons for all of us. It is also a source of comfort and encouragement for everyday living. It is such a blessing when God opens my eyes to new truths each time I read it. His Word is Awesome.

One of my special joys is time spent with my grand children. Traveling by air, which I also love, to a distant state where some of them live, as well as my two special grandsons who live near by. I have been blessed with a son and a daughter who love the Lord and teach their children the importance of a right relationship to God and to our Lord Jesus Christ.

I belong to a senior citizens group called the XYZ club. It stands for “extra years of zest” which most of us could interpret as extra years of hoping we will make it through each day. We really have lots of fun with the support of our canes and walkers while we tune up our hearing aids and get ready for a gab fest. We meet together once each month for lunch and just getting to know each other. Being part of this group helps makes my life interesting.

I hope you will enjoy our chats on this site and that you will make comments or share things that interest you.
