Revealing God’s Word

On this page I want to share with you God’s blessings on my life in and through the experiences, the trials, the heartaches and victories over my many years of walking with Him. There are many precious, as well as fun and serious things that have influenced me in my daily life which I treasure and want to tell you about. I especially want to share God’s leading as He teaches me the joys of dependence on his Grace and Mercy in all things through His Word.

It will be my joy to share with you with the importance of living in awesome wonder of Godâ€™s great love for us. To always be aware of his presence, in every part of our lives. I had always dreamed of being a writer. This has been the fulfillment of that dream, as God has enabled me to do even these short stories.

I cannot help but marvel at the beauty and simplicity of all of God’s Word and his plans for each of our lives. When we see His complete provision for all our daily needs, both materially and spiritually, it makes living for Him a priority. God bless you as you join me in this – my privilege of sharing his wonderful Word and His goodness and blessings.
