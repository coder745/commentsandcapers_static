Cleansing Power

1 John 1:9

I took a look at my dingy worn out deck that had been built years ago and decided a new one was needed. As I watched the old one taken down and replaced by a beautiful new one I was extremely proud and satisfied. It was a joy and a pleasure to behold. Then it happened. Extreme heat, Wind, rain and dirt soon made it look dirty and old.. I was disappointed but learned that it could be restored to make it look good again. What it needed was to be washed. Not just with water and cleaning ingredients but with something more powerful.

Then one day my two my teenage grandsons came over with a power washer we thought might fix the problem. Besides having fun it was amazing what they could do with it. Dirt and grime just seemed to vanish, as the powerful flow worked its way across the wood. It began to look like new. Its power even made the sidewalk look white again.

What in the world happened to your sidewalk? It looks like someone has painted it and your deck looks great too. This question and comment came in the place of the usual “Hi” when I had a visitor the next morning.

My plan now was to apply whatever it took to keep it looking beautiful and clean. Before we could apply protection it began to rain and did not stop. The wood stayed wet for days making it impossible to apply water seal to it. It stayed wet until it was too late to stop the damage. It was amazing what a power washer could do but it could not guarantee a permanent job. My new deck is in need of cleansing again.

We are a lot like that wood. We may look beautiful on the outside but beauty is only skin deep. The years of splashing through the mud and dirt of sin take their toll and our self-effort to make our lives presentable to God is ineffective.

Water will not wash away sin and rebellion against God. Even if we make ourselves look good for awhile we have no protection from the temptations of sin, unless we apply the true power washer. It is the one that can do a permanent job of cleansing from the inside out.

There is no greater cleansing than this. It starts with the heart where its powerful flow brings cleansing, peace, joy and satisfaction. For this we must accept the cleansing power of the shed blood of the Lord Jesus Christ which he promised in 1 John 1:9.

Try it! When you do you will probably hear comments like, “what in the world happened to you?”
