Real Stability

Psalm 139:14

“When I woke up this morning I realized that there was something strange about my room. I could not focus my eyes on the wall that seemed to be moving. Even the floor was tilted. It was a very awkward feeling when I needed to get up and walk. My physical need to get somewhere in a hurry was hampered considerably. I am sure I was a strange sight as I grabbed the bedpost to keep from falling. My first step was a disaster. I fell over backwards and bruised my pride. The night stand gave a part of my anatomy something to remember too. Ouch it really hurt. My self control took a beating and I was a mess.

I was home alone so I had no help. I tried again and worked my way to the door and latched on. It was then a wall to wall struggle to get down the hall. Thankfully there were no stairs to deal with. I finally reached the kitchen only to find my medicne bottle, that I kept for this type of emergency, was empty. Feeling helpless, I gave way to tears. I have never enjoyed having company find me in such a situatuion but this time an unexpected visitor was a blessing.

The ability of the body to keep itself properly oriented or positioned is called equilibrium. It gives us bodily stability or balance. When it is not working correctly sometimes instead of balance it throws us a curve and makes us walk sideways and feel dizzy. This is not only unpleasant but is pretty risky. It can put us on the floor in a heartbeat and bruise our ego along with other things. Thankfully there is a precription that will help.

God gave us balance in the body because it is necessary for our well being but that is not enough. We need spiritual balance also.The balance needed for a spiritual walk is provided by the grace of God. Once we were groping our way through life searching for the medicine that would make our life balanced. Satan was throwing us curves to get us confused. With nothing to grasp but empty promises we stumbled along. Nothing would satisfy but the medicine that gives real peace to the soul.

We do not have to keep searching. The medicine was given long ago at Calvary. It is not a pill, it is a life. The life that Jesus Christ gave for us when he died on the cross. Faith in Him alone brings balance in our body, soul and Spirit.

“If any man be in Christ, he is a new creature: Old things are passed away; behold all things are become new. “2 Cor. 5:17. “For in Him we live, and move, and have our being, Acts 17:28.

Let go of the bed post of Satan’s deception. Latch on to the real stability of God’s gift of grace and enjoy the assurance of eternal life. Believing in Christ we can enjoy peace and contentment and the assurance of salvation that makes life worth living. Accept the love of God offered in John 3:16. You will be glad you did.

I have found that the– “best part of waking up”– is to be able to rise,make up my bed and walk.
